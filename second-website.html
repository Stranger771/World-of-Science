What is Science?

Science fundamentally is the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the natural and physical world through observations and experiments. Study of science evolved with the civilization of human beings.

Science is the study of nature.Science is divided into
many branches. Some example are given
below.

Biology

The study of living things.

Chemistry

The study of matter.

Physics

The study of energy and natural forces.

Geology

The study of the structures of the Earth.

Astronomy

The study of stars,planets,moons and other objects or phenomena in outer space.

Technology

What is Technology?

Science has been applied to make new products and solve problems. The application of science is known as Technology. The Internet, computer chips, man-made satellites, genetically
modified food, and cloning of animals are
all products of technology

Benefit of Science and
Technology.

Science and technology have brought great benefits to mankind. Science and technology have enabled us to grow more food, live longer,make our lives more comfortable,communicate faster and farther, move from place to place with ease and explore outer space.

Abuses and Limitation of
Science and Technology.

However, science and technology can bring about negative effects as well. For example the generation of electricity using fossil fuels can lead to pollution which causes harm to human beings and the environment. Also the invention and use of deadly weapon can harm us and our environment.

Nasa

NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration. NASA was started on October 1, 1958. Founder of Nasa was Dwight D. Eisenhower. And now
the Administrator of Nasa is Jim Bridenstine.

Astronauts in orbit conduct scientific research. Satellites help scientists learn more about Earth.
Space probes study the solar system and beyond. New developments improve air
travel and other aspects of flight. The agency shares what it learns so that its
information can make life better for people worldwide. For example,
companies can use NASA discoveries to creat spinoff products.
